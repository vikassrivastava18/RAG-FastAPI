Fusion is an AI-powered educational support tool developed to assist faculty with instructional design, course planning, and content development. It primarily generates responses using openly licensed materials from Open. However, with limited exception, it may draw from external sources only when generating case studies.

By using Fusion, faculty acknowledge and agree to the following:

  • 1. Primary Source: Open
    All responses are based on Open titles, except in the case of case study development. When creating case studies, Fusion may pull general information from publicly available internet sources to add realistic context.
  • 2. Faculty Oversight Required
    All content generated by the AI—especially case studies—should be reviewed by faculty to ensure accuracy, relevance, and alignment with course outcomes and institutional standards.
  • 3. Instructional Support Only
    Fusion is a tool for educational support and is not intended to replace faculty judgment, academic expertise, or compliance with institutional policies.
  • 4. Content Accuracy and Limitations
    While we strive to ensure quality, AI-generated outputs may be incomplete, outdated, or imprecise. Critical evaluation and professional discretion are essential before using content in academic settings.
  • 5. Ethical Use and Data Practices
    Use of Fusion implies agreement to responsible use in instructional contexts. User interactions may be reviewed to improve platform functionality in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
